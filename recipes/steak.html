Pan Seared Steak

A perfectly pan seared steak is a thing of beauty, crispy golden-brown crust on the outside, tender and juicy on the inside, bursting with rich, buttery flavor. The high heat sear locks in the juices, while garlic and fresh herbs infuse every bite with mouthwatering depth. It's the kinf of steak that makes your kitchen smell like a high end steakhouse and has you savoring every forkful like it's a special occasion.

Ingredient List

  • 1 well-marbled steak (ribeye,filet mignon, or New York strip)
  • Salt (coarse or flaky)
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1-2 Tbsp high smoke point oil (avocado oil or vegetable oil)
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted buttery
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1-2 sprigs fresh rosemary or thyme
